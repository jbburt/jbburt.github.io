About

I'm a computational scientist. My primary interests -- in no particular order -- include physics, mathematics, computer science, neuroscience, bioinformatics, and psychiatry. My grad school research in the Murray Lab focused on computational modeling of neural circuit dynamics in human cortex. I currently work at Neumora Therapeutics as a data scientist.
